HTTP/3 — QUIC at the Transport Layer

What Changed

  • Built on QUIC (UDP) with streams that avoid transport head-of-line blocking.
  • TLS 1.3 integrated; faster handshakes (often 0-RTT).
  • QPACK header compression designed for HTTP/3’s stream model.

Why It Matters

Improves performance on lossy/mobile networks and is widely supported by modern browsers and CDNs.
